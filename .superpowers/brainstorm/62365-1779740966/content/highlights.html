四类高亮的视觉样式

文章里的标注应该长什么样？

Markets are efficient because risks cancel out in large portfolios. Idiosyncratic risks are unique to each asset, unlike systematic risks, which affect all assets simultaneously and cannot be eliminated through diversification, a concept explored through Modern Portfolio Theory.
▬ 关键论点 ▬ 生词 ╌ 复杂句 ┄ 延伸概念

A — 底线 + 淡背景

每种类型用不同颜色底线区分，配淡色背景。低视觉噪音，类型一目了然。

Markets are efficient because risks cancel out in large portfolios. Idiosyncratic risks are unique to each asset, unlike systematic risks, which affect all assets simultaneously and cannot be eliminated through diversification, a concept explored through Modern Portfolio Theory.
关键论点 生词 复杂句 延伸概念

B — 彩色填充标签

每类高亮用深色背景 + 亮色文字。视觉对比强，类型区分清晰，但长句标注会影响阅读节奏。

Markets are efficient because risks cancel out in large portfolios. Idiosyncratic risks are unique to each asset, unlike systematic risks, which affect all assets simultaneously and cannot be eliminated through diversification, a concept explored through Modern Portfolio Theory.
— 关键论点 — 生词 — 复杂句 — 延伸概念

C — 纯底线，无背景

最克制。只用颜色底线，完全不遮挡文字。阅读体验最干净，但类型区分需要记住颜色含义。
